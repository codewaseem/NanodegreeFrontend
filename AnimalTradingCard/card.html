Goldfish

A layer of mucus on the goldfish's skin makes it immune to the fish-eating anemone's lethal sting.

  • Scientific Name: Carassius auratus
  • Average Length: 10 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 5 years
  • Habitat: Meditteranean coral reefs

Bright orange goldfish are among the most recognizable of all reef-dwellers. They are popular pets as well. They reach about 10 centimeters in length, and are named for the multicolored sea anemone in which they make their homes.
